Foundation Validated

Manual flower inventory management leads to spoilage, missed deliveries, and operational chaos

The Problem

Florist shop owners face critical operational bottlenecks: perishable inventory expires without proper tracking, custom bouquet orders get lost in manual spreadsheets, delivery schedules conflict and miss time-sensitive windows, and valuable flowers spoil before sale—directly cutting into profit margins and damaging customer trust.

The Solution

Florist shop owners juggle multiple challenges: tracking perishable flower stock across multiple storage conditions, coordinating complex bouquet orders with custom arrangements, managing delivery schedules manually, and ultimately losing revenue to wilted inventory and missed delivery windows. Without visibility into flower freshness and order priorities, shops struggle to optimize fulfillment, waste precious product, and disappoint customers.

Target Audience

Independent and small-chain florist shop owners managing 5–50+ daily orders

Personal Motivation

Create a calm, organized operational backbone that lets florists focus on creativity and customer relationships instead of logistics chaos
