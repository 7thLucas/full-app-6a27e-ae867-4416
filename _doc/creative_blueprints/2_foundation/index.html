The Problem

Running a Florist Shop Is Harder Than It Looks

Perishable Inventory

Flowers expire in days. Over-order and you waste money. Under-order and you lose sales — there's no margin for error.

Fragmented Operations

Paper order books and spreadsheets don't connect inventory to orders — or orders to deliveries. Nothing talks to anything.

Missed & Late Deliveries

Without coordinated scheduling, arrangements arrive late or wilted — damaging customer trust when it matters most.

Industry Reality

15–25% of perishable inventory is wasted in the average florist shop.

The Solution

One Platform. Everything Connected.

BloomOps brings inventory, orders, and deliveries into a single coherent system — with freshness-first intelligence that catches spoilage risk before it costs you.

Freshness Tracking

Per-variety arrival dates and expiry alerts before flowers go to waste.

Order Pipeline

From intake to doorstep — every order fully tracked, every step of the way.

Delivery Scheduling

Calendar, driver assignments, and area routing — fully coordinated.

Spoilage Intelligence

At-risk stock surfaced proactively — act before flowers go to waste.
